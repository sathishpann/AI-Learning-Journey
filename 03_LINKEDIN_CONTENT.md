## ✍️ Writing Tips

### Hook (First Line)
Strong openings that stop the scroll:
- "X months ago I [starting point]. Today I [achievement]."
- "🎯 Just shipped: [impressive thing]"
- "I spent X hours learning AI. Here's what happened..."
- "From [beginner state] to [expert state]: My journey"

### Body
- Use emojis sparingly (2-4 per post max)
- Short paragraphs (2-3 lines each)
- Bullet points for lists
- Include specific numbers and metrics
- Tell a mini-story (problem → solution → result)

### Call-to-Action
End every post with engagement:
- "What would you build with this?"
- "DMs open for questions"
- "Check out the code: [link]"
- "Who else is learning AI? Let's connect"
- "What should I build next?"

### Hashtag Strategy
Use 5-8 relevant hashtags:
- **Broad:** #AI #MachineLearning #Python
- **Specific:** #Anthropic #ClaudeAPI #ModelContextProtocol  
- **Career:** #CareerDevelopment #SoftwareEngineering #LearningInPublic
- **Action:** #100DaysOfCode #BuildInPublic #TechTwitter
