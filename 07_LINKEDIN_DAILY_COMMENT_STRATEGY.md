# LinkedIn Daily Comment Strategy (30 Days)

Goal: increase visibility, relevance, and inbound profile views while you run your 30-day posting calendar.

Time budget: 20 minutes per day
- 10 minutes: comment on other creators
- 5 minutes: reply to comments on your own post
- 5 minutes: send one thoughtful connection request

## Comment Formula

Use this 3-part structure:
1. Signal agreement or insight in one sentence.
2. Add one practical example from your enterprise architecture or AI learning journey.
3. End with one question that continues the conversation.

Example:
"Great point on AI governance. In my enterprise environment, adding human-in-the-loop approval improved trust quickly. How are you measuring adoption beyond raw usage metrics?"

## Who To Comment On Daily

Prioritize these groups:
1. AI engineering practitioners
2. Enterprise architects discussing modernization
3. Cloud and DevOps leaders
4. Data engineering and analytics leaders
5. Recruiters hiring AI, architecture, or platform roles

## Weekly Cadence

### Week 1
Focus: establish presence
- Comment on 3 AI/GenAI posts daily
- Comment on 2 enterprise modernization posts daily

### Week 2
Focus: domain authority
- Add one mini case example in at least 2 comments each day
- Ask one strong follow-up question in every comment

### Week 3
Focus: relationship building
- Revisit people who replied to you
- Add follow-up comments to continue threads
- Send 1 connection request daily with context

### Week 4
Focus: conversion
- Comment on hiring and role-trend posts
- Reference your active repository where relevant:
  https://github.com/sathishpann/AI-Learning-Journey
- Invite conversation on enterprise AI implementation
